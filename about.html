About Krypt

Welcome to Krypt, your ultimate crypto portfolio tracker. We are passionate about helping you manage your digital assets and investments in the world of cryptocurrencies. Krypt was born out of a desire to make the complex world of blockchain and crypto more accessible, manageable, and enjoyable for every investor, from newcomers to seasoned traders.

At Krypt, we believe in the power of data, visualization, and user-friendly tools to empower you to make informed investment decisions. Our app is designed with a commitment to excellence and innovation in mind, and it's built to help you succeed in the ever-evolving crypto landscape.

Our Features

  • Portfolio Tracking: Krypt allows you to effortlessly keep tabs on your crypto investments. You can add your assets, no matter how diverse or unique, and see their performance at a glance. Our user-friendly interface makes tracking your portfolio simple and efficient.
  • Data Visualizations: We understand that data can be overwhelming. That's why we offer a range of visualizations to help you interpret your holdings, including asset distribution, price distribution, and line charts showing the performance of your crypto over the past 7 days. These visuals provide invaluable insights into your investments.
  • Top Cryptos: Stay updated on the latest trends in the cryptocurrency world. Krypt highlights the top-performing cryptocurrencies and keeps you informed about market movers and shakers.
  • Customization: We respect the individuality of every investor. Krypt lets you customize your portfolio, add the assets you hold, and monitor your unique investment strategy.
